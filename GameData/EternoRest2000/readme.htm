Eterno-Rest 2000 (REST)

Tragic disasters happen in the world of rocketry now and then, give your highest achieving Kerbals the burial they deserve with this pressurized coffin!

By zer0Kerbal, originally by Aphtonites with Acea_

Preamble by Acea_

All honors to Aphtonites, author of the original Eterno-Rest 2000 on SpacePort.

This is an UNOFFICIAL update version of that mod, a simple coffin model, used as a memorial of your brave Kermans who got KIA or MIA.

Original version description by Aphtonites

Tragic disasters happen in the world of rocketry now and then, give your highest achieving Kerbals the burial they deserve with this pressurized coffin!

Once you get it into space (and hopefully in a circular orbit) you can rename the satellite it’s mounted on to the name of the Kerbal who passed away, so they’ll never be forgotten.
